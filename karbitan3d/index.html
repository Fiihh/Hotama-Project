About Karbitan3D

Karbitan3D is a small team focused on creating anime-style 3D character models. We accept orders for fanart, OC models, as well as for VTuber and 3D printing purposes.

With experience in sculpting, retopology, and rigging, we are ready to help you bring your dream character to life in high quality 3D. Come on, collaborate with us!
